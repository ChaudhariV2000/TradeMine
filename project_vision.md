# TradeMine AI

## Vision

Build an AI-powered investment manager that helps retail investors
make disciplined, explainable, and continuously improving investment
decisions.

TradeMine does not simply recommend stocks.

It manages the complete investment lifecycle.

---------------------------------------------------------

The AI decides.

The user configures constraints.

---------------------------------------------------------

## Principles

1. Capital Preservation First

Never force a trade.

Holding cash is a valid decision.

---------------------------------------------------------

2. Explain Every Decision

Every recommendation must include

- Technical reasons
- Fundamental reasons
- News impact
- Risk
- Market regime
- Preferred strategy

---------------------------------------------------------

3. Continuous Learning

Every paper trade becomes training data.

Retraining happens only after evaluation.

Models improve gradually.

---------------------------------------------------------

4. Portfolio First

Never optimize one stock.

Optimize the portfolio.

---------------------------------------------------------

5. Dynamic Market Selection

The AI chooses where to search.

Not the user.

---------------------------------------------------------

6. Risk Before Return

No recommendation without

- Position sizing
- Stop loss
- Target
- Risk Reward

---------------------------------------------------------

7. Fully Automated Research

Every market day

Download data

↓

Analyze

↓

Rank

↓

Allocate

↓

Paper Trade

↓

Monitor

↓

Learn

---------------------------------------------------------

8. Modern User Experience

Simple

Fast

Visual

Explainable

Never overwhelm the investor.

---------------------------------------------------------

9. Modular AI

Each agent has one responsibility.

Technical

News

Fundamental

Risk

Market

Strategy

Decision

Learning

Portfolio

---------------------------------------------------------

10. Production Quality

No shortcuts.

Everything should be scalable.
